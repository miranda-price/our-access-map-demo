Service Request

Fill out this form to send a service request to Facilities and Services at the University of Rochester. For fire, flood, or other emergencies, please contact the Department of Public Safety at x5-333333 or 585-275-3333.

Alternatively, you can email University Facilities & Services at faccust@facilities.rochester.edu to place a service request. Be sure to include your name, phone number, location, and a description of the request. You can also call at x3-4567 or 237-4567.
